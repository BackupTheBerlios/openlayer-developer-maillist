Oh yeah I'm going to replace all the C strings with C++ ones most definitely. 
I just fleshed that out real quick so I can have a framework to build off of.
Those #defines I pulled from GK I plan to get rid of them, well the PI I can 
keep. And most of the other GK stuff I pulled into Glyph. The friend 
functions will remain until Glyph is completely functional.
I plan to make the Glyph class represent a complete font and will contain 
everything you need to transform, modify and render with it. That way you 
don't have a confusing assortment of different structures. FreeType is not 
that complicated to begin with and we don't need to support C.
If you want or are going to need access to specific Glyph's I can do so. I'm 
still learning the API so it's going to be a bit before I can actually do 
advanced stuff with FreeType.
