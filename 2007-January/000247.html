Hi, 

I have just (accidentally) discovered that OpenLayer is licensed under LGPL - at least that is what the official site at berlios says. 

There's a problem here. 

With LGPL, if you include the files into your project, even by using a static library, your own project becomes infected with the LGPL, thus forces to release your own sourcecode under LGPL. The only way out would be to build a DLL, so that the binary can be used. 

I'm not sure if that is what you intended, and would ask you to check this issue. Maybe change the license to one that is less restrictive (the allegrogl project gives you the choice to use GPL or zlib, whatever you like better). I really would like to use OpenLayer for my own project, but I would not be able to use it with LGPL... 

Thanks!
